About Us


Are you seeking an extraordinary winter wonderland, where nature's beauty takes on a magical form? Look no further than our breathtaking Snow Forest! Nestled in the heart of a pristine winter landscape, our destination promises an unforgettable journey into a world of awe-inspiring serenity and wondrous adventures.

Step into a surreal realm where snow-laden trees reach for the sky, and every step you take is cushioned by a soft carpet of pristine snow. Our Snow Forest offers a captivating escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life, allowing you to immerse yourself in the peace and tranquility of nature's snowy embrace.

Services


Thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike will find an array of exhilarating activities in the Snow Forest. Embark on a thrilling snowshoeing expedition, forging your own path through the winter wonderland. Grab your skis or snowboard and navigate the slopes, gliding through powdery snow and feeling the adrenaline rush with every turn.
For a more tranquil experience, embark on a serene sleigh ride, pulled by majestic horses as you relish the breathtaking scenery. Or why not build a snowman or engage in an epic snowball fight with family and friends, creating memories that will last a lifetime?

After a day filled with adventure, relax and rejuvenate in our cozy retreats nestled within the Snow Forest. Whether it's a luxurious cabin, a charming lodge, or a snug winter cottage, our accommodations provide the perfect sanctuary for unwinding. Warm up by a crackling fireplace, sip hot cocoa while gazing at the starlit sky, or simply bask in the peaceful ambiance of the snowy surroundings

Snow Forest is home to an array of playful and curious creatures that add charm and delight to your wildlife adventure. Watch the energetic antics of the mischievous snowshoe hare, as it hops through the snow, leaving behind its distinctive tracks. Catch sight of the elusive pine marten, expertly maneuvering through the treetops with acrobatic finesse. And if you're lucky, you might even spot the rare and adorable Arctic fox, its fluffy white coat perfectly adapted to the frosty environment.


Capture rare and awe-inspiring moments with your camera as you come face-to-face with the inhabitants of Snow Forest. Freeze in time the powerful gaze of a snow leopard, the grace of a reindeer in motion, or the fluffy charm of a playful snowshoe hare.
